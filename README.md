## Analysis Performed
- Total number of sales and customers  
- Category-wise sales and transactions  
- Average age of customers by category  
- High-value transactions analysis  
- Monthly sales trends and best-performing months  
- Top 5 customers based on total spending  
- Unique customers per category  
- Sales distribution by time shifts (Morning, Afternoon, Evening)  

## Key Insights
- Sales vary significantly across product categories  
- Certain months show higher average sales, indicating seasonality  
- A small group of customers contributes to high revenue  
- Time-based analysis shows variation in transaction patterns

This project demonstrates how SQL can be used for effective data analysis and business insight generation.
